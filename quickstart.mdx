## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in and create a workspace">
    Go to [app.ravell.io](https://app.ravell.io) and sign in with your email or Google. If you're joining an existing team, accept the workspace invitation from your email.

    A **workspace** is your team's shared space. If you're the first from your team, create a new workspace and give it a name. If you received an invitation, accept it to join an existing workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your first source">
    1. Open **Sources** in the sidebar (or **Settings → Integrations**).
    2. Click **Connect** next to Linear, Notion, Intercom, Slack, or Attio.
    3. Complete the OAuth flow in the popup.
    4. For Linear: select which teams to sync. For Slack: select which channels to index.

    Ravell starts syncing your data. The more sources you connect, the better your answers.

    <Tip>
      Start with the tool your team uses most. You can add more sources anytime.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask your first question">
    1. Click **New Ask** in the sidebar or use the main input.
    2. Type a question in plain language, e.g. "What did customers say about checkout last month?" or "What are the top open issues in our current cycle?"
    3. Press Enter. The answer streams in with citations you can click to see the source.
    4. Ask follow-up questions in the same thread for deeper context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your first agent">
    1. Open **Agents** in the sidebar and click **Create agent**.
    2. Pick a template — **Weekly product roundup** or **Daily standup prep** are great starting points.
    3. Customize the question and schedule (e.g. every Monday at 9am).
    4. Save. Click **Run now** to see the report immediately in your **Inbox**.

    <Tip>
      Enable **Deliver to Slack** to have reports posted to a channel automatically.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
