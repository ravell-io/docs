## Overview

Connect **Intercom** to Ravell to search across your customer conversations. Ravell indexes conversations so you can analyze feedback themes, track satisfaction, identify churn signals, and understand what customers are asking for.

---

## Connect Intercom

1. Go to **Sources** or **Settings → Integrations**.
2. Click **Connect** next to Intercom.
3. Sign in to Intercom and authorize Ravell.
4. Select which teams or inboxes to sync (if applicable).
5. Ravell starts syncing. Initial sync may take a few minutes.

---

## What you can ask

| Question | What Ravell does |
|----------|------------------|
| "What did customers say about checkout last month?" | Clusters customer feedback by theme |
| "Summarize feedback about pricing" | Extracts and synthesizes pricing-related conversations |
| "What are the top support issues this week?" | Identifies trending support topics |
| "Which customers mentioned churn or cancellation?" | Finds conversations with churn signals |
| "What feature requests came in from enterprise accounts?" | Filters feedback by account type and topic |
| "How satisfied are customers with onboarding?" | Analyzes satisfaction signals from conversations |
| "Show me escalated conversations from last week" | Finds high-priority or escalated conversations |
| "What competitors did customers mention?" | Searches for competitive mentions in customer conversations |

## Customer feedback analysis patterns

Intercom is one of Ravell's most powerful sources for understanding customer sentiment. Try these workflows:

**Theme clustering**: "Cluster customer feedback from the last 30 days into themes with counts" — groups similar feedback and shows volume.

**Trend tracking**: "How has customer sentiment about [feature] changed over the last 3 months?" — identifies shifts in feedback over time.

**Cross-source analysis**: "What feature requests from Intercom are already in our Linear roadmap?" — connects customer demand to planned work.

---

## What gets synced

- **Conversations**: Messages, timestamps, participants, tags.
- **Customer context**: Linked to conversations where relevant.

Ravell uses this data to answer questions about customer feedback, support issues, and product requests.

---

## Re-sync and reindex

Ravell syncs periodically. Trigger a manual **Reindex** or **Sync** from **Sources** to refresh data after bulk imports or major changes.
