## Overview

Connect **Intercom** to Ravell to search across your customer conversations. Ravell indexes conversations so you can ask questions like "What did customers say about checkout last month?" or "Summarize feedback from Intercom about pricing" and get answers with citations.

---

## Connect Intercom

1. Go to **Sources** or **Settings â†’ Integrations**.
2. Click **Connect** next to Intercom.
3. Sign in to Intercom and authorize Ravell.
4. Select which teams or inboxes to sync (if applicable).
5. Ravell starts syncing. Initial sync may take a few minutes.

---

## What gets synced

- **Conversations**: Messages, timestamps, participants, tags.
- **Customer context**: Linked to conversations where relevant.

Ravell uses this data to answer questions about customer feedback, support issues, and product requests.

---

## Use cases

- "What did customers say about [feature] last month?"
- "Summarize feedback from Intercom about pricing."
- "What are the top support issues this week?"

---

## Re-sync and reindex

Ravell syncs periodically. You can trigger a manual **Reindex** or **Sync** from **Sources** to refresh data after bulk imports or major changes.
