## Overview

Connect **Attio** to Ravell to bring CRM context into your product intelligence. Ravell indexes your notes, meeting transcripts, call recordings, deals, records, and threads so you can ask questions like "What meetings did we have with Acme Corp?" and get answers that combine CRM data with your other sources.

---

## Why connect Attio?

Attio data enriches Ravell's answers with customer relationship context:

- **Meeting notes and transcripts** give context about customer conversations
- **Deal information** connects revenue context to product feedback
- **Contact records** link people across Intercom conversations, Linear issues, and Slack discussions
- **Call recordings** surface what was discussed verbally, not just in text

When Attio is connected alongside Intercom, Ravell can connect customer support conversations to sales interactions, giving you a complete picture of each account.

---

## Connect Attio

1. Go to **Sources** or **Settings → Integrations**.
2. Click **Connect** next to Attio.
3. Sign in to Attio and authorize Ravell.
4. Select which objects and data to sync.
5. Ravell starts syncing. Initial sync may take a few minutes.

---

## What gets synced

| Data type | What's included |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Notes** | Notes attached to companies, contacts, and deals |
| **Meeting transcripts** | Transcriptions of recorded meetings |
| **Call recordings** | Call transcription content |
| **Deals** | Deal names, stages, values, and associated context |
| **Records** | Company and contact records with attributes |
| **Threads** | Internal discussion threads on records |

---

## Example questions

| Question | What Ravell does |
|----------|------------------|
| "What meetings did we have with Acme Corp?" | Finds meeting transcripts linked to that company |
| "Show me recent notes on the Series B deal" | Retrieves deal-related notes and activity |
| "What did we discuss in calls with enterprise prospects last month?" | Searches call recordings and transcripts by time range |
| "Which deals closed last month?" | Lists recently closed deals with context |
| "What do we know about [company name] across all tools?" | Combines Attio notes with Intercom conversations, Linear issues, and Slack mentions |

---

## Cross-source value

Attio is most powerful when combined with other sources:

- **Attio + Intercom**: Connect sales context to support conversations. Ask "What did Acme Corp discuss in both sales calls and support tickets?"
- **Attio + Linear**: Link customer requests from CRM notes to product issues. Ask "Which deals mention features we haven't built yet?"
- **Attio + Slack**: Combine internal discussions with CRM data. Ask "What did the team discuss about the Acme deal?"

---

## Re-sync and reindex

Ravell syncs Attio data periodically. Trigger a manual **Reindex** or **Sync** from **Sources** to refresh data after adding new notes, closing deals, or importing records.
