## Overview

Evidences are indexed excerpts from your connected sources (Linear, Notion, Intercom, Slack). The API lets you search evidence directly for integrations or custom UIs.

---

## Search evidence (workspace)

`GET /api/v1/workspaces/:workspace_id/evidences/search`

Searches evidence within a workspace.

**Query parameters**:

- `q`: Search query.
- `source_ids`: Optional. Limit to specific integration IDs.
- `limit`: Optional. Max results (default varies).

**Response**: List of evidence items with `id`, `content`, `source`, `metadata`, `url`, etc.

---

## Get full evidence

`GET /api/v1/workspaces/:workspace_id/evidences/:id/full`

Returns the full content of a single evidence item (e.g. full Linear issue or Notion page).

---

## Document evidences

For document-specific evidence (e.g. within a plan or Notion export):

`GET /api/v1/documents/:document_id/evidences`

`POST /api/v1/documents/:document_id/evidences`

`DELETE /api/v1/documents/:document_id/evidences/:id`

Manage evidence linked to a specific document.
