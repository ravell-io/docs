## Sync not completing

**Symptom**: A source shows "Syncing" for a long time or gets stuck.

**Cause**: Large workspaces with thousands of items can take time on initial sync. Network issues or expired OAuth tokens can also cause stalls.

**Fix**:
1. Check the sync status in **Sources** — look for error messages.
2. If stuck, try clicking **Sync** or **Reindex** to trigger a manual refresh.
3. If errors persist, disconnect and reconnect the integration to refresh the OAuth token.

## Empty or irrelevant answers

**Symptom**: Ravell returns "I couldn't find relevant information" or gives an answer that doesn't match your question.

**Cause**: The question may be too vague, the data may not be synced yet, or the answer might be in a source you haven't connected.

**Fix**:
1. Be more specific — add time ranges, team names, or source hints. See [Better questions](/better-questions).
2. Check that the relevant source is connected and synced in **Sources**.
3. Try scoping to a specific source: "from Linear only" or "from Intercom only."
4. If the data was just added, trigger a manual sync and try again.

## Slack Agent not responding

**Symptom**: You mention @Ravell or use `/ravell` but don't get a response.

**Cause**: The Slack app may not be installed in that channel, or Slack may not be connected as a source.

**Fix**:
1. Confirm Slack is connected in **Sources** or **Settings → Integrations**.
2. Make sure the Ravell app is added to the channel you're messaging in.
3. Try a DM to @Ravell to confirm the bot is active.
4. If the app is installed but not responding, disconnect and reconnect Slack.

## OAuth re-authorization needed

**Symptom**: A source shows an error or stops syncing, and the fix is to re-authorize.

**Cause**: OAuth tokens can expire or be revoked (e.g. if someone changed permissions in the integration's admin panel).

**Fix**:
1. Go to **Settings → Integrations**.
2. Disconnect the affected integration.
3. Reconnect it — this generates a fresh OAuth token.
4. Reselect teams/channels if prompted.

## Agent runs failing

**Symptom**: An agent shows "Failed" status in your Inbox.

**Cause**: The question may reference data that no longer exists, a source may be disconnected, or there may be a temporary processing error.

**Fix**:
1. Check the error message on the failed run (click to expand).
2. Verify all referenced sources are still connected and synced.
3. Try clicking **Run now** to trigger a manual retry.
4. If the question references specific items (like a cycle that ended), update the question to use relative time ranges (e.g. "current cycle" instead of a specific date).

---

## Profile not updating

**Symptom**: Your profile shows stale information that doesn't reflect recent changes.

**Cause**: Profiles are auto-generated and cached. Changes in your connected sources may not be reflected until the next refresh.

**Fix**:
1. Open **Context** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Refresh** to regenerate your profile from current source data.
3. You can also manually override specific sections if the auto-generated content isn't right.

---

## "No results found" errors

**Symptom**: Ravell says it can't find any results for your question.

**Cause**: The search terms don't match any indexed content, the time range is too narrow, or the relevant data hasn't been synced.

**Fix**:
1. Broaden your question — remove time constraints or source scoping.
2. Try different wording — Ravell searches by meaning, so rephrasing can help.
3. Check that the relevant source is connected and synced.
4. Verify the data exists in the original tool (e.g. confirm the issue exists in Linear).
