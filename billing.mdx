## Overview

Ravell billing is managed per workspace from **Settings → Billing**. You can view your current plan, track usage, upgrade, and manage your subscription.

---

## Plans

Ravell offers two plans:

| Feature | Free | Pro |
|---------|------|-----|
| Web Asks | Limited | Included |
| Agents | Limited | Unlimited |
| Sources | All supported | All supported |
| Slack Agent | Included | Included |
| Team members | Limited | Unlimited |

For current plan details and limits, visit **Settings → Billing** in the app.

---

## Usage tracking

The billing page shows your current usage for the billing period:

- **Asks usage bar** — a visual bar showing how many Asks you've used relative to your plan limit. The bar turns amber when you reach 80% of your limit.
- **Daily activity chart** — a bar chart showing your Asks per day across the current billing period. Use this to spot usage trends and plan upgrades.

---

## Upgrade or change plans

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to billing">
    Open **Settings → Billing** in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a plan">
    Choose the plan you want and click **Subscribe** or **Upgrade**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    Complete the checkout process to activate your new plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Your workspace is updated automatically. New limits and features are available immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Only workspace **owners** and **admins** can upgrade plans or manage billing.
</Note>

## Customer portal

The customer portal lets you:

- Update your payment method
- View invoices and payment history
- Change plan or cancel subscription
- Update billing details

Access it from **Settings → Billing → Manage subscription**.

---

## Workspace billing

Billing is per workspace. Each workspace has its own subscription and usage limits. If you belong to multiple workspaces, each is billed separately.

The billing page also shows:

- **Members on plan** — the number of billable members in your workspace.
- **Current period end date** — when your billing period resets.
