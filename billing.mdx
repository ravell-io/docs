## Overview

Ravell uses **Polar** for billing. You can manage plans, checkout, and access the customer portal from Settings.

---

## Plans

Ravell offers different plans to fit your team's needs. Plan features, limits, and pricing are shown when you sign up or in **Settings → Billing**.

| Feature | Free | Pro |
|---------|------|-----|
| Web Asks | Limited | Unlimited |
| Agents | Limited | Unlimited |
| Sources | All supported | All supported |
| Slack Agent | Included | Included |
| Team members | Limited | Unlimited |

For current pricing details, visit **Settings → Billing** in the app.

---

## Upgrade or change plans

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to billing">
    Open **Settings → Billing** in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a plan">
    Choose the plan you want and click **Subscribe** or **Upgrade**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    You're redirected to Polar's checkout. Complete payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Your workspace is updated automatically. New limits and features are available immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customer portal

The **Customer portal** lets you:

- Update payment method
- View invoices and payment history
- Change plan or cancel subscription
- Update billing details

Access it from **Settings → Billing → Manage subscription**. You'll be redirected to Polar's hosted portal.

---

## Query limits and usage

Each plan includes a per-user query limit. When you approach or reach your limit, Ravell shows a usage indicator in the app.

- **Free plan** — A limited number of queries per user per billing period.
- **Pro plan** — Higher or unlimited queries per user per billing period.

To check your current usage:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Your usage (queries used vs. limit) is shown in the billing status section.

Usage resets at the start of each billing period. If you hit your limit, upgrade your plan or wait for the next period.

### Usage history

**Settings → Billing** also shows a daily usage breakdown for the current billing period, so you can see how your query volume trends over time.

---

## Workspace billing

Billing is per workspace. Each workspace has its own subscription. If you have multiple workspaces, each is billed separately. Only workspace **owners** and **admins** can manage billing.
