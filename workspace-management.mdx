## Overview

Workspace management covers members, invitations, and roles. Owners and admins can manage these from **Settings**.

---

## Members

**Settings → Members** shows all workspace members with their roles and status.

- **Change role**: Owners can promote or demote members between Admin and Member roles.
- **Suspend**: Temporarily revoke a member's access. Suspended members can be unsuspended later without needing a new invitation.
- **Remove**: Permanently remove a member from the workspace.

### Import from Slack

If you have Slack connected, you can import workspace members directly from Slack instead of inviting them one by one. Click **Import from Slack** on the Members page to sync your Slack team roster into Ravell.

---

## Invitations

**Settings → Invitations** (or **Members → Invite**) lets you invite people by email. A badge appears on the workspace picker when you have pending invitations to review.

- **Send invitation**: Enter an email address and select a role (Admin or Member). The invitee receives an email with a link.
- **Pending invitations**: View and cancel pending invites.
- **Accept/Decline**: Invitees use the link in the email to accept or decline. Once accepted, they become members. Invitations can also be accepted from the workspace picker dropdown.

---

## Roles

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| **Owner** | Full access. Can manage members, billing, invitations, integrations, and delete the workspace. |
| **Admin** | Can manage members and invitations, change roles, and configure workspace settings. Cannot manage billing or delete the workspace. |
| **Member** | Can use Asks, Agents, Inbox, Sources, and Context. Cannot manage other members or billing. |

Owners and admins can change roles and manage invitations.

## Team onboarding checklist

When onboarding your team to Ravell, follow this checklist:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up the workspace">
    Create the workspace and connect your most-used sources (Linear, Intercom, Slack, Notion, Attio). See [Workspace setup](/workspace-setup).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the workspace profile">
    Open **Context** and fill in the workspace profile: company mission, product context, goals, and ICP. This personalizes answers for everyone. See [Profiles](/profiles).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team">
    Go to **Settings → Members → Invite** and add teammates by email — or use **Import from Slack** if you have Slack connected. Start with 2-3 people to pilot, then expand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share a starter question">
    Send teammates a specific question to try first, like "What are the top open issues in our current cycle?" along with a link to the [Quickstart](/quickstart).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up a shared agent">
    Create a shared agent (e.g. **Weekly product roundup**) and deliver it to a team Slack channel. This gives passive value to people who haven't tried asking yet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Workspace settings

**Settings → Workspace** (or **General**) lets you:

- Update workspace name.
- Set your company website URL (used for profile enrichment — Ravell uses this to generate more accurate workspace context).
- Complete onboarding (if applicable).
- Delete workspace (owners only — requires typing the workspace name to confirm).

<Warning>
  Deleting a workspace permanently removes all data including sources, Asks, Agents, members, and reports. This cannot be undone.
</Warning>
