---
title: "Agent templates"
description: "Pre-built agent templates for common product intelligence workflows"
---

Templates give you a head start when creating agents. Each template pre-fills a question, schedule, and description — customize before saving.

Open **Agents** → **Create agent** to browse the gallery.

---

## Execution templates

Templates for tracking work progress, alignment, and priorities.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Weekly product roundup">
    **Question**: "Summarize the top issues and feature requests from Linear this week."

    **Value**: Stay on top of your backlog without manual triage.

    **Default schedule**: Every Monday at 9:00am

    **Customize**: Add specific teams or projects to focus the roundup. Include "and customer feedback from Intercom" for cross-source context.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Daily standup prep">
    **Question**: "What customer feedback came in yesterday?"

    **Value**: Start every standup with fresh customer context.

    **Default schedule**: Every weekday at 8:00am

    **Customize**: Change to "What happened in [team name] since yesterday?" for team-specific standups. Add "Focus on completed work, new issues, and blockers" for a more structured report.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Product priorities gap">
    **Question**: "What should we be working on today that we aren't?"

    **Value**: Align work with what matters most.

    **Default schedule**: Every Monday at 9:00am

    **Customize**: Scope to specific teams or add "based on customer feedback and roadmap" for evidence-backed prioritization.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Project misalignment signals">
    **Question**: "Identify projects we're actively working on and surface evidence we may be building the wrong thing (customer feedback, support tickets, or internal doubts)."

    **Value**: Surface evidence you're building the wrong thing.

    **Default schedule**: Every Monday at 9:00am

    **Customize**: Focus on specific projects by name for targeted alignment checks.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Roadmap vs. reality">
    **Question**: "Compare our stated roadmap (Linear initiatives/projects) to recent customer feedback. Where are we out of sync?"

    **Value**: Keep strategy grounded in customer feedback.

    **Default schedule**: Every Monday at 9:00am

    **Customize**: Best run bi-weekly. Add "Focus on enterprise customer feedback" to weight by segment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Stale work detection">
    **Question**: "Which Linear projects or issues have had no activity for 2+ weeks but are still marked in progress?"

    **Value**: Clear bottlenecks and dead work.

    **Default schedule**: Every Monday at 9:00am

    **Customize**: Adjust the staleness threshold ("1 week", "3 weeks") to match your team's cadence.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Customer templates

Templates for monitoring customer health, satisfaction, and growth signals.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Churn signals">
    **Question**: "Which accounts showed churn risk indicators in the last 7 days?"

    **Value**: Catch at-risk accounts before they leave.

    **Default schedule**: Every Friday at 5:00pm

    **Customize**: Deliver to your CS team's Slack channel for immediate visibility. Add "Include context from Attio notes" if Attio is connected.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Support trends">
    **Question**: "Summarize Intercom ticket trends and common themes this week."

    **Value**: Spot recurring issues before they scale.

    **Default schedule**: Every Monday at 10:00am

    **Customize**: Add "Compare to last week" for trend tracking. Focus on specific product areas if needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Most unsatisfied customers">
    **Question**: "Which customers showed the strongest dissatisfaction with the product in the last 30 days, and what are the main reasons?"

    **Value**: Know who's struggling and why.

    **Default schedule**: Every Monday at 9:00am

    **Customize**: Filter by customer segment or product area for targeted insight.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Most satisfied customers">
    **Question**: "Which customers showed the strongest satisfaction with the product, and what do they value most?"

    **Value**: Know who loves you and what they value.

    **Default schedule**: Every Monday at 9:00am

    **Customize**: Use this to identify case study candidates and reference customers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Expansion signals">
    **Question**: "Which customers mention needing more seats, new use cases, or additional products? Surface expansion opportunities."

    **Value**: Turn happy customers into growth opportunities.

    **Default schedule**: Every Monday at 9:00am

    **Customize**: Deliver to your sales or CS channel for follow-up action.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Champion identification">
    **Question**: "Which customers or contacts consistently advocate for us or give positive feedback? What do they value?"

    **Value**: Find advocates for case studies and referrals.

    **Default schedule**: Every Monday at 9:00am

    **Customize**: Run monthly for a broader view of advocacy patterns.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Escalation patterns">
    **Question**: "Which Intercom tickets escalated or took multiple rounds? What patterns do they share?"

    **Value**: Learn from tickets that needed extra attention.

    **Default schedule**: Every Monday at 9:00am

    **Customize**: Add "and suggest process improvements" for actionable insights.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Using templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse">
    Open **Agents** → **Create agent** to see all available templates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select">
    Click a template to pre-fill the question and schedule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize">
    Edit the question to match your team's needs. Adjust the schedule, timezone, and visibility.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and run">
    Save the agent. Click **Run now** to see the first report immediately, or wait for the next scheduled time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You don't have to start from a template. Click **Create agent** and write any question from scratch. Templates are just shortcuts for common patterns.
</Tip>
