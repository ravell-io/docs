## What are Sources?

**Sources** are the tools you connect to Ravell via OAuth: Linear, Notion, Intercom, Slack, and Attio. Ravell syncs data from these sources, indexes it for search, and uses it to answer your questions with citations.

**Why it matters:** The more sources you connect, the richer and more accurate your answers. Ravell searches across all connected sources simultaneously, merges results, and follows links between documents across tools. You get answers even when the right info is scattered across different tools.

---

## Connect a source

1. Open **Sources** in the sidebar (or **Settings → Integrations**).
2. Click **Connect** next to Linear, Notion, Intercom, Slack, or Attio.
3. Complete the OAuth flow in the popup. Authorize Ravell to read the data you need.
4. For **Linear**: Select which teams to sync. For **Slack**: Select which channels to index.
5. Ravell starts syncing. Initial sync can take a few minutes depending on data volume.

### Which sources should I connect first?

Start with the tool your team uses most. Here's a quick guide:

| If you want... | Connect first |
|----------------|---------------|
| Issue and project tracking | **Linear** |
| Customer feedback and support | **Intercom** |
| Team discussions and decisions | **Slack** |
| Documentation and specs | **Notion** |
| CRM and sales context | **Attio** |

Each source adds value on its own, but the real power comes from cross-source questions. Connect 2-3 sources to start seeing connections.

## Sync status

In **Sources**, you can see the status of each connection:

- **Synced**: Data is up to date.
- **Syncing**: Sync in progress.
- **Error**: Sync failed. Check the connection and re-authorize if needed.

Use **Reindex** or **Sync** to refresh data manually when needed.

---

## Managing indexed documents

The **Sources** view shows a summary of all indexed documents across your connected integrations. You can:

- **View summary**: See the total number of indexed documents, broken down by source type and integration.
- **Browse documents**: View individual indexed documents with their sync status, source, and metadata.
- **Toggle sync**: Enable or disable syncing for individual documents. Disabled documents are excluded from search results.
- **Reindex**: Trigger a manual reindex of a specific document to refresh its content.
- **Delete**: Remove a document from the index entirely.

For Notion, you can also **batch-add pages** — select multiple Notion pages at once to start indexing them.

---

## Scope queries to sources

When asking questions, you can scope to specific sources:

- **All sources** (default): Ravell searches across everything you've connected.
- **Specific source**: Limit the search to one tool (e.g. "Linear only") for faster, more focused answers.

Scoping is useful when you know the answer is likely in one place.

---

## Disconnect a source

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations** (or **Sources**).
2. Find the integration and click **Disconnect**.
3. Ravell stops syncing and removes the data from its index. Past Ask answers that cited that source will still show the citations, but the underlying data may no longer be available.
