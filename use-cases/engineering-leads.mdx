As an engineering lead, you need visibility across teams, cycles, and customer impact. Ravell gives you evidence-backed status updates without chasing teammates or reading every issue.

---

## Top workflows

### Cycle reviews

Get a comprehensive review of what was accomplished, what slipped, and why.

**Ask**: "Summarize what each team shipped in the last cycle and what rolled over"

Ravell searches Linear for completed and incomplete issues across teams, groups by team, and highlights rollover patterns.

**Follow-up**: "Which rolled-over issues are high priority?"

---

### Stale work detection

Find issues that have gone quiet and may need attention.

**Ask**: "What issues haven't been updated in more than 2 weeks?"

Ravell identifies stale issues across all teams, sorted by age.

**Suggested agent**: Set up a **Stale work** agent weekly to catch forgotten items automatically.

---

### Bug triage

Track bug trends and identify systemic issues.

**Ask**: "What bugs were filed vs resolved this week across all teams?"

**Follow-up**: "Which areas have the most open bugs?"

**Follow-up**: "Are any of these bugs mentioned by customers in Intercom?"

---

### Cross-team dependencies

Spot dependencies that could block progress.

**Ask**: "Show me cross-team dependencies in the current cycle"

**Follow-up**: "Which of those are blocking?"

---

### Standup prep

Prepare for standup in 2 minutes instead of 20.

**Ask**: "What happened in the backend team since yesterday? Focus on completed work, new issues, and blockers"

**Suggested agent**: Set up a **Daily standup prep** agent every weekday at 8:30am, delivered to your team's Slack channel.

---

## Suggested agents

| Template | Schedule | What you get |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| **Daily standup prep** | Every weekday | Yesterday's completions, new issues, blockers |
| **Stale work** | Every Monday | Issues with no activity in 14+ days |
| **Weekly product roundup** | Every Friday | What shipped across teams this week |
| **Misalignment detector** | Every Monday | Gaps between stated priorities and active work |

---

## Example questions

- "What's blocking the most issues right now?"
- "Compare velocity across teams this cycle"
- "Show me tech debt issues filed this month"
- "What customer-facing bugs are open?"
- "Which issues are assigned but not started?"
- "What did the frontend team discuss in Slack this week?"

---

## Getting started

1. Connect **Linear** (required). Add Slack for team discussion context and Intercom for customer impact.
2. Set up your [individual profile](/profiles) with your team and areas of ownership.
3. Create a **Standup prep** agent for your team channel.
4. Try weekly cycle reviews by asking about sprint progress every Friday.
