## By complexity

### Simple lookups

These are fast, direct retrievals:

- "Find ENG-142"
- "What's the status of Project X?"
- "Find the onboarding spec in Notion"
- "Show me my assigned issues"

### Analysis

These require searching, filtering, and synthesizing:

- "What are the top 5 support issues this week?"
- "Summarize feedback about pricing from the last 30 days"
- "Which issues are blocked and why?"
- "What stale work hasn't been updated in 2 weeks?"

### Synthesis

These combine information across multiple sources:

- "What shipped last week and how did customers react?"
- "Connect customer feedback to our current roadmap"
- "Compare progress across all teams this cycle"
- "Create a weekly product roundup with customer context"

### Comparisons

These contrast or compare data:

- "Compare bug rates between the mobile and web teams"
- "How has customer sentiment about pricing changed over the last 3 months?"
- "What's the gap between what we planned and what we shipped?"
