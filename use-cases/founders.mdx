As a founder, you need to stay informed across product, engineering, and customers without getting buried in tools. Ravell gives you a single source of truth across all your teams' work.

---

## Top workflows

### Weekly company pulse

Get a complete picture of what's happening across the company in one answer.

**Ask**: "Give me a weekly pulse: what shipped, what's blocked, top customer feedback, and upcoming priorities"

Ravell searches Linear, Intercom, Slack, and Notion to compile a cross-functional summary.

**Suggested agent**: Set up a **Weekly product roundup** agent every Monday at 8am for an automated start to your week.

---

### Roadmap vs reality

Check if the team is building what matters most.

**Ask**: "How does our current roadmap compare to what customers are asking for?"

Ravell identifies gaps between planned features in Linear and customer requests in Intercom, highlighting demand that isn't addressed.

**Follow-up**: "Which customer requests have the highest volume?"

---

### Customer discovery

Understand your market directly from customer conversations.

**Ask**: "What are the top themes from customer conversations this month?"

**Follow-up**: "How do enterprise customer themes differ from SMB?"

**Suggested agent**: Set up a **Discovery gaps** agent to surface recurring customer patterns bi-weekly.

---

### Investor update prep

Compile product highlights and customer metrics for investor communications.

**Ask**: "Summarize our product progress and key customer metrics for the last month"

**Follow-up**: "Push this to Notion in our Investor Updates database"

---

### Competitive intelligence

Monitor competitive mentions across your tools.

**Ask**: "What competitive mentions appeared in customer conversations and Slack discussions this month?"

Ravell searches Intercom and Slack for mentions of competitors and clusters them by theme.

## Example questions

- "What are the 3 biggest risks to our roadmap right now?"
- "What did customers say about our latest release?"
- "How many bugs vs features shipped this month?"
- "What are the top requests from our ICP accounts?"
- "Summarize what every team accomplished this week"
- "What decisions were discussed in leadership Slack channels?"

## Getting started

1. Connect **all available sources** — Linear, Intercom, Slack, Notion, and Attio. Founders benefit most from cross-tool context.
2. Set up the [workspace profile](/profiles) with your company's mission, ICP, and key goals.
3. Create a **Weekly product roundup** agent delivered to your leadership Slack channel.
4. Use cross-source questions to connect the dots across teams and customers.
