As a CS professional, you need to spot risks early, track satisfaction, and connect customer feedback to product progress. Ravell searches across Intercom, Linear, Slack, and Attio to give you the full picture.

---

## Top workflows

### Churn signal detection

Identify accounts showing signs of disengagement or dissatisfaction.

**Ask**: "Which accounts showed churn signals in the last 7 days?"

Ravell searches Intercom and Attio for conversations mentioning cancellation, frustration, or reduced usage patterns.

**Suggested agent**: Set up a **Churn signals** agent every Friday to monitor at-risk accounts weekly.

---

### Satisfaction tracking

Understand how customers feel about specific features or the product overall.

**Ask**: "How satisfied are customers with our onboarding experience based on recent Intercom conversations?"

Ravell clusters feedback by sentiment and theme, showing what's working and what isn't.

**Follow-up**: "Which specific customers were most frustrated?"

---

### Champion identification

Find your happiest customers who could be advocates or references.

**Ask**: "Which customers have given positive feedback about our product in the last month?"

**Follow-up**: "Which of those are enterprise accounts?"

---

### Escalation management

Stay on top of escalated issues and their resolutions.

**Ask**: "Show me escalated Intercom conversations from this week and their current status"

**Follow-up**: "Which of these have related Linear issues?"

---

### Expansion opportunities

Spot signals that suggest upsell or expansion potential.

**Ask**: "Which customers asked about features available in higher-tier plans?"

Ravell identifies conversations where customers expressed interest in features they don't currently have access to.

---

## Suggested agents

| Template | Schedule | What you get |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| **Churn signals** | Every Friday | Accounts with cancellation or frustration mentions |
| **Support trends** | Every Monday | Top themes from Intercom conversations |
| **Most unsatisfied customers** | Every Friday | Accounts with the most negative satisfaction signals |
| **Escalation patterns** | Every weekday | New escalations needing attention |

---

## Example questions

- "What are the top customer complaints this month?"
- "Which enterprise accounts haven't had contact in 30 days?"
- "Summarize feedback about our API from the last quarter"
- "What feature requests come up most in expansion conversations?"
- "Show me positive feedback to use in a case study"
- "Which customers mentioned competitors recently?"

---

## Getting started

1. Connect **Intercom** (required). Add Attio for CRM context, Slack for internal discussion, and Linear for product progress.
2. Set up your [individual profile](/profiles) with your role and accounts you manage.
3. Create a **Churn signals** agent to run weekly.
4. Use cross-source questions to connect customer feedback with product roadmap.
