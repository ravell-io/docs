## How it works

**Sources** are what Ravell knows — connect Linear, Notion, Intercom, Slack, and Attio via OAuth. **Asks** are one-off Q&A conversations with citations. **Agents** are scheduled questions that deliver reports to your **Inbox** and optionally to Slack.

All three use the same evidence-backed pipeline: intent classification, multi-source search, evidence ranking, synthesis with citations, and verification. Connect sources once; use them everywhere.
