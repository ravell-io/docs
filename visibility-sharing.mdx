## Private vs shared

Visibility controls who can see your content. Three types of content have visibility settings:

- **Asks** — Private Asks are only visible to you. Shared Asks appear in the sidebar for all workspace members.
- **Agents** — Private Agents and their reports are only visible to you. Shared Agents are visible to the workspace.
- **Folders** — Private folders organize your own Asks. Shared folders are visible to workspace members.

---

## Set visibility when asking

Before submitting your first question, use the visibility toggle (lock/globe icon) next to the input. Click to switch between private and shared. The default depends on your workspace settings.

---

## Change visibility later

You can change an Ask's visibility after it exists:

1. Open the Ask.
2. Click the **•••** (options) menu on the answer card or in the sidebar.
3. Choose **Make private** or **Share with workspace**.

---

## Copy link

To share a direct link to an Ask:

1. Open the Ask.
2. Click the **•••** menu.
3. Choose **Copy link**.

The link opens the Ask directly. Recipients need access to the workspace to view shared asks.

---

## Workspace visibility

Shared Asks show workspace member avatars in the sidebar so you can see who else has access. Private Asks show only to you.

## Folder visibility

Folders have their own visibility setting, independent of the Asks inside them. When you create or edit a folder, choose **private** or **shared**. Moving an Ask into a shared folder does not automatically change the Ask's own visibility.
