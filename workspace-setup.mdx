## What is a workspace?

A **workspace** is your team's shared space in Ravell. All sources (Linear, Notion, Intercom, Slack), Asks, Agents, and Inbox reports live in a workspace. Members share access to the same data and can collaborate on questions and reports.

---

## Create a workspace

1. Sign in at [app.ravell.io](https://app.ravell.io).
2. If you don't have a workspace yet, you'll be prompted to create one.
3. Enter a name (e.g. "Acme Product Team") and confirm.
4. You become the **owner** of the workspace.

---

## Invite members

1. Open **Settings** (gear icon) and go to **Members**.
2. Click **Invite member**.
3. Enter their email address.
4. They receive an invitation email with a link to join.

Members can **accept** or **decline** the invitation. Once they accept, they can access the workspace and all its sources, Asks, and Agents.

---

## Roles

Workspace members have roles that control what they can do:

- **Owner**: Full access. Can manage members, billing, and delete the workspace.
- **Member**: Can use Asks, Agents, Inbox, and Sources. Cannot manage billing or remove other members.

Roles are managed in **Settings → Members**. Owners can change a member's role or suspend/remove them.

---

## Onboarding

After creating a workspace, the owner is guided through a three-step onboarding flow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect internal tools">
    Connect at least one internal tool — **Slack**, **Linear**, or **Notion**. These provide the core data Ravell indexes for your team. Select which channels, teams, or pages to sync during setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect customer sources">
    Optionally connect customer-facing tools like **Attio**, **Intercom**, or additional Slack/Linear/Notion sources. You can skip this step and add them later from **Sources**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get started">
    You're shown a welcome screen with your two primary surfaces — **Web Asks** and **Slack Agent** — and can jump straight into asking your first question.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can always add or remove sources later from the **Sources** view.

---

## Switch workspaces

If you belong to multiple workspaces, use the workspace picker in the sidebar to switch. Each workspace has its own sources, Asks, and Agents.
